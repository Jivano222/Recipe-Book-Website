Ingredients - Brownies

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/3 cup cocoa
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Ingredients - Icing

  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp cocoa
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup icing sugar

Procedure

  • Preheat oven to 350Â°F
  • To create the icing just combine each of the ingredients in a separate bowl
  • Butter and flour the brownie pan
  • Melt butter on low heat in saucepan
  • Remove from heat and add sugar, egg, vanilla, cocoa, flour, salt, & baking poweder
  • Pour brownie batter into brownie pan
  • Add icing to brownie while hot
